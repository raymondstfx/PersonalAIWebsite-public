# Publications

## Identifying Cortical Molecular Biomarkers Potentially Associated with Learning in Mice Using Artificial Intelligence

- Authors: Huang, X., Gauthier, C., Berger, D., Cai, H., & Levman, J.
- Author role: Xiyao Huang is the first author of this publication.
- Journal: International Journal of Molecular Sciences
- Year: 2025
- Volume/Issue: 26(14)
- Article: 6878
- DOI: https://doi.org/10.3390/ijms26146878
- Summary: This study used machine learning and feature selection to identify cortical molecular biomarkers potentially associated with learning in mice. The work used public-domain data and reproducible machine learning methods. Six key protein biomarkers were identified as highly predictive in the study context: BDNF, NR2A, BCL2, H3AcK18, pERK, and SOD1.
