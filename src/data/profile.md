# Profile

Xiyao Huang is a Master of Information Technology student at the University of New South Wales with a computer science background across China, Canada, and Australia. His academic and project experience focuses on machine learning, biomedical data analysis, computer vision, efficient AI models, and applied AI systems.

His work includes a first-author biomedical machine learning publication on cortical molecular biomarkers associated with learning in mice, an honours thesis on effective crowd counting using deep learning networks, evolutionary computation projects, the Personal AI Website project, and industry experience involving data platforms, recommendation workflows, and internal AI tools.

Xiyao is interested in PhD and research opportunities related to machine learning for precision medicine, biomarker discovery, disease outcome prediction, biomedical imaging, interpretable AI, and efficient AI systems for research applications.
